What happens next

  1. You submit this form with the bucket name from your CFN Outputs.
  2. Blacktip generates your Ed25519-signed license bound to your CFN stack's installation ID.
  3. We upload refine.license (~500 bytes) to your delivery bucket via AWS S3.
  4. The license-watcher on your EC2 picks it up within 60 seconds and restarts the container.
  5. Refine's UI is live ~30 seconds after that. You'll receive an email from AMelcher@blacktip-ops.com with your activation code.

SLA: response within 1 business hour during US business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm ET). Questions? Email corporate@blacktip-ops.com.
