Request your Refine license

You've subscribed to Refine on AWS Marketplace and launched the CloudFormation stack. Send us your DeliveryBucketName and we'll upload your Ed25519-signed license file within one business hour.

What happens next

  1. You submit this form with your CFN Outputs and the IDs of every cloud account Refine will monitor.
  2. Blacktip generates your Ed25519-signed license bound to your CFN stack's installation ID and the cloud accounts you listed.
  3. We upload refine.license (~500 bytes) to your delivery bucket via AWS S3.
  4. The license-watcher on your EC2 picks it up within 60 seconds and restarts the container.
  5. Refine's UI is live ~30 seconds after that. You'll receive an email from AMelcher@blacktip-ops.com with your activation code.

Have these ready

  • AWS Marketplace customer ID (from the subscription confirmation email)
  • The CFN stack DeliveryBucketName from the Outputs tab
  • For AWS: 12-digit account IDs + whether each is Commercial or GovCloud
  • For Azure: subscription UUID + tenant UUID + whether it's Azure Public or US Government
  • For GCP: project ID + (optional) organization ID + whether it's Standard or Assured Workloads (Government)

SLA: response within 1 business hour during US business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm ET). Questions? Email corporate@blacktip-ops.com.
